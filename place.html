About Aruba

Aruba is a tiny Dutch Caribbean island located just 18 miles north of Venezuela. Famous for its flamingos, white-sand beaches, crystal-clear turquoise waters, and nearly constant trade winds, this 70-square-mile desert island offers year-round sunshine, vibrant coral reefs, and a perfect blend of relaxation and adventure.
